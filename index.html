ABOUT SKN IEEE STUDENT BRANCH(SKN ISB)

SKN IEEE Student Branch was formed in 2021. We started gaining traction in 2022 and have been consistent in conducting various events since then.
Even though our student branch is quite new, we still have made our mark.
Currently, IEEE SKNCOE serves over 21 active student members and is growing fast.
